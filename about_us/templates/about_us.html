About Us

Happy Little Artists Art Club is a place where creativity thrives and young minds flourish. Founded in 2024 by Amanda, a passionate and experienced primary school teacher, our art classes offer a delightful journey into the world of imagination and self-expression. Nestled in Ballyowen Castle Community Center, we cater to children aged 5-10. Class Structure

At Happy Little Artists Art Club, we follow an exciting and enriching class structure designed by Amanda. Our classes, held over ten-week periods, provide children with a foundation in the elements of art: line, shape, colour, tone, texture, form, and space. Amanda's approach is fun, engaging, and accessible to all children, regardless of their artistic background. Each term builds upon these elements, allowing children to explore and express their creativity freely. Through art, we aim to develop not only their artistic skills but also essential life skills such as confidence, self-esteem, concentration, and patience. {% if user.is_authenticated %} Join us {% else %} Join us {% endif %} and watch your child fall in love with art, gaining valuable experiences that will last a lifetime!
